## CRITICAL FIX — Music silent-tail bug (compositor) [fixed, NOT yet deployed]
- **Symptom found during Phase 3 audio QA**: every reel ended in DEAD SILENCE for 1.6–5.7s. Audio stream length ≈ VO length, NOT the target reel length (e.g. Reel2: 15s video but audio only 9.29s = 5.7s silent tail).
- **Root cause**: in lib/compositor.ts voice+music branch, `sidechaincompress` only emits output while BOTH inputs have signal, so the ducked music `[mduck]` ended exactly when the VO (sidechain key) ended. When the pipeline extends the final scene past the VO to hit target duration T, the music died with the VO → silent video tail.
- **Fix**: pad the sidechain key with silence to T before sidechaincompress: `[voice]...asplit=2[vo][vokeyraw]; [vokeyraw]apad,atrim=0:T,asetpts=PTS-STARTPTS[vokey];` then `[musbed][vokey]sidechaincompress...[mduck]; [mduck]atrim=0:T,...afade...[musf]`. Once VO stops, key is silent → no compression → music gently swells back to bed level and sustains under the held outro frame, then fades over last 0.8s. Also padded the voice-only branch (`apad,atrim=0:T`).
- **PROOF (3 regenerated reels, all audio == video length)**: Reel1 15.0/15.0, Reel2 15.0/15.0 (was 9.29), Reel3 25.0/25.0. Outro music (VO absent) now audible: R1 −30.0dB mean/−19.3 peak, R2 −23.1/−12.2, R3 −25.9/−11.0; final fades −33 to −41dB (no hard cut). VO peaks −4.8 to −10dB sit clearly above ducked bed.
