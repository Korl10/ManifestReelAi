Fixes shipped (`lib/generation-pipeline.ts`, `lib/quota.ts`, `lib/providers/flux-image.ts`, admin margins, Craft wizard, `lib/captions/ass.ts`):
- **Hard-fail guardrail:** motion-tier reel with `motionClipCount===0` → status='failed', coinCost=0, full `refundCoins()`, `[ALERT][motion-fail]` log, job errorMessage "Cinematic engines were busy — your reel was not charged…", early return (no wasted voice/composite spend). `motionVerified = motionClipCount>0`.

- **Cinematic animates ALL scenes:** `maxMotion = tier==='cinematic' ? sceneCount : 4` (kills slideshow feel).
- **Subject lock:** `flux-image.ts` generates scene 0 then uses flux redux (`fal-ai/flux-pro/v1.1-ultra/redux`, image_prompt_strength 0.18) referencing scene 0 for scenes 1-N. Toggle "Lock subject across scenes" in Craft Visual step (default ON). Schema: `BrandPreset.subjectLock` (default true).
- **refundCoins(userId, amount, reason):** decrements usageCounter.reelsUsed first, remainder as non-expiring CoinPurchase bonus (bundleId=`refund:<reason>`). NOT idempotent — guard with persisted flag.
